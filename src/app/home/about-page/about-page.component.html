About us

DuboTech Story

DuboTech is a pioneering deep tech startup from Bangladesh, specializing in cutting-edge underwater technology. The company boasts a team with a wealth of experience and expertise in developing innovative propulsion systems, advanced sensors, and autonomous navigation solutions. With a mission to commercialize underwater technology, DuboTech aims to generate revenue while continuously advancing its technological capabilities. Positioned to cater to the growing demand for underwater robotics, the company envisions playing a pivotal role in the global marine industry, offering services that are firmly rooted in artificial intelligence and engineering excellence. This strategic focus on DuboTech signifies a dynamic leap forward in its journey to become a leading force in the realm of deep underwater technology. Discovering Underwater Possibilities.

In the heart of Bangladesh, where countless ponds and fisheries shimmer under the sun, a transformative journey begins. At DuboTech, we've always been driven by the spirit of innovation, and we're taking that spirit beneath the waves.

VISION & MISSION

We envision to become a global leader in providing customizable automated inspection solutions for onshore and offshore submerged structures. Furthermore, DuboTech intends to excel as a leading product and services company in the domain of Under-fluid Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) for long-term asset integrity monitoring across industries.
